AxisShieldLogo.png Overview
The AxisShield logo combines elements of security and surveillance, symbolizing the project's focus on securing and backing up camera feeds. The design incorporates a shield and a stylized camera lens.

Iteration Notes:
- The concept aimed to reflect the essence of AxisShield without infringing on existing trademarks.
- The design emphasizes modernity, professionalism, and relevance to technology and security.
